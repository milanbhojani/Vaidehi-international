About Vaidehi International

At Vaidehi International, we focus on maintaining the highest standards of quality in our products as well as our daily business operations. Our advanced production unit and warehouse are equipped with state-of-the-art technology, enabling us to manufacture and supply a wide range of precisely engineered polymer granules.

We are committed to providing our customers with the best wholesale prices without compromising on quality. Our dedication to 100% customer satisfaction is reflected in our comprehensive services, from manufacturing to global shipping. With Vaidehi International, you can trust that you are partnering with a company that values excellence, innovation, and reliability.

Welcome to Vaidehi International, one of the fastest growing newly formed organizations in India, dedicated to supplying premium quality polymer granules. With a strong commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the polymer industry over the past 10 years.

  • Quality Focus: Maintaining the highest standards of quality in products and business operations is our main priority.
  • Advanced Production Unit: Our state-of-the-art production unit ensures manufacturing excellence.
  • Global Reach: We ensure reliable global shipping for all our polymer products, meeting the demands of customers worldwide.
  • Best Seller Products: We offer a diverse range of granules including Green ABS Plastic Granules, Grey HIPS Granules, Brown EPS Granules, Transparent GPPS Granules, Yellow ABS Plastic Granules, and more
